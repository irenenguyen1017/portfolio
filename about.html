Who am I?

Time for a career change?

Hi, I am Irene Nguyen. I am 34 and currently studying a Diploma of Information Technology at Coder Academy. Before moving to Australia, I studied and worked in Japan for 12 years. I am a native Vietnamese speaker, fluent in Japanese but still learning English. Working as a Human Resources Consultant in Japan, I had the opportunity to travel around Japan on business. It was a wonderful time that I will never forget. However, since having babies, I want to change my career to a job where I can work from home and have flexible hours. As a result, I chose to become a web developer.

What skill do I have?

I have basic knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I took some online lessons and read some books to understand how they work behind the scenes.

What skills am I aiming for?

I know there are many programming languages, and no one knows all of them. I found that Coder Academy delivers the most used programming languages, such as Python, JavaScript, React.js, and more. I aim to master all of them and strongly believe that I can demonstrate my abilities to prospective employers.
